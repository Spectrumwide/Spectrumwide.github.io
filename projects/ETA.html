Abstract



The Email Tone Analyser is an innovative application portal that leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to analyze the tone of email communications. Built using React for the frontend and Node.js for the backend, this application provides users with insights into the emotional content of their emails, helping them to better understand and manage their communication style. The portal offers a user-friendly interface where users can input their email text and receive real-time analysis of the tone, sentiment, and emotional cues present in the content. By utilizing advanced NLP algorithms, the Email Tone Analyser can identify various tones such as positive, negative, neutral, formal, informal, and more. This analysis can be particularly useful for professionals, customer service representatives, and anyone looking to improve their email communication skills.



Motivation


The motivation behind developing the Email Tone Analyser stems from the growing importance of effective communication in both personal and professional settings. Emails are a primary mode of communication, and the tone of an email can significantly impact how the message is perceived by the recipient. Misinterpretations of tone can lead to misunderstandings, conflicts, and missed opportunities. By providing users with insights into the tone of their emails, the Email Tone Analyser aims to enhance communication effectiveness, foster better relationships, and promote a more empathetic approach to digital correspondence.




Description

The Email Tone Analyser application portal is designed to provide a seamless user experience while delivering accurate and insightful tone analysis. The frontend, built using React, offers a clean and intuitive interface where users can easily input their email text. The application features a text area for users to paste or type their email content, along with a submit button to initiate the analysis process. Upon submission, the frontend communicates with the backend, developed using Node.js, which handles the NLP processing and tone analysis. The backend utilizes various NLP libraries and algorithms to analyze the email text, identifying key emotional cues and sentiment indicators. The results of the analysis are then sent back to the frontend, where they are displayed in an easy-to-understand format. Users can view detailed insights into the tone of their email, including visual representations such as graphs or charts to illustrate the emotional distribution within the text. The UI is divided into several sections, Home, input area, analysis results, about us and Documentation.


Note :

The NLP model is not yet trained and this is a prototype. The analysis results are dummy data and the focus is on UI/UX design.

Case Study

Abstract



The Bank Management System is a comprehensive web application developed using React, designed to simplify and enhance banking operations for both customers and administrators. The system offers features such as efficient account management, real-time transaction tracking for deposits, withdrawals, and transfers, and robust authentication mechanisms to ensure secure login and safeguard user data. With a user-friendly and responsive interface, it provides seamless navigation across devices. Additionally, the admin dashboard enables administrators to monitor and manage user accounts, transactions, and system settings effectively. This project showcases the integration of modern web technologies to deliver a secure, scalable, and user-centric banking solution.



Motivation


The motivation behind creating the Bank Management System was to address the challenges faced by traditional banking systems, such as inefficiency, lack of user-friendly interfaces, and security concerns. By leveraging modern web technologies, the goal was to provide a seamless, secure, and efficient banking experience for both customers and administrators.



This project aims to bridge the gap between traditional banking operations and the digital era, ensuring accessibility and convenience for all users.


Description

The portal interface of the Bank Management System is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, featuring seamless access across devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones and Robust authentication mechanisms to protect user data and a centralized interface for users to view account details, transaction history, and perform banking operations.


Login


The login page of the Bank Management System provides a secure authentication process for users to access their accounts. It includes fields for entering the username and password, along with options for password recovery and account registration. The login page is designed to be responsive and user-friendly, ensuring a seamless login experience for users.


Registration


The registration page allows new users to create an account on the Bank Management System. It includes fields for entering personal details, contact information, and account preferences. The registration process is designed to be user-friendly and secure, with validation checks and error handling to ensure accurate data entry. Upon successful registration, users can log in to their accounts and access the banking features provided by the system.



My Profile


The My Profile section of the Bank Management System allows users to view and update their personal information, contact details, and account preferences. Users can edit their profile information settings as needed. The My Profile section is designed to provide users with easy access to manage their account details and ensure data accuracy and security.



My Account

The My Account section of the Bank Management System provides users with an overview of their account details, including account number, balance, transaction history, and account status. Users can view recent transactions for record-keeping. The My Account section is designed to offer users a comprehensive view of their banking activities and account status.



Apply Loan

The Apply Loan feature of the Bank Management System allows users to apply for loans online. Users can select the loan type, enter the loan amount, and provide additional details for loan processing. The Apply Loan feature is designed to simplify the loan application process and provide users with a convenient way to access financial services.

Customer will be asked to select between Education and personal loan based on the Loan option selected the customer will be asked to enter the loan amount and the tenure of the loan and the system will calculate the EMI based on the tenure and the loan amount entered by the customer.


Once the customer clicks on the Apply Loan button the system will display the EMI amount and the customer can click on the Apply button to apply for the loan. The system will display the message that the loan has been successfully applied and the customer can check the loan status in the My Account section.
